Founder and creator

Sammit Basu

Sammit founded placeholder.com to make websites that feel less like pages and more like places.

He brings creative direction, interaction design, 3D thinking, and front-end engineering into one continuous process. That means the original idea survives all the way from the first conversation to the final browser window.

placeholder.com stays intentionally independent. Clients work directly with the person shaping the concept and building the experience, with trusted specialists brought in when a project needs photography, sound, writing, or complex 3D production.
